GE 498 - Senior Design Project

Student Learning Objectives

Students who complete GE 498 should be able to:

  1. Function effectively as a member of a multidisciplinary team
  2. Build a physical prototype of a design based on engineering drawings, flowcharts, and circuit schematics
  3. Effectively present oral progress reports
  4. Write a technical document to summarize your design, prototype, and test results
  5. Prepare and present an effective technical oral report to summarize your work
  6. Use design specifications and a test plan to evaluate the success of a physical prototype
  7. Incorporate engineering standards into your design project
  8. Create quality audio/visual materials to support oral presentations and written reports
  9. Identify important parameters to be measured to check if your design requirements are met
  10. Synthesize and integrate previous knowledge in mathematics, science, and engineering to assist in the achievement of a successful design
  11. Prepare a budget and cost analysis of your project
